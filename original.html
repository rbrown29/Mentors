Get to know your online faculty mentor

Contact Greg Kaminski

Online faculty mentors at PCC offer guidance through the online course development and course revision process. They help train new online instructors and help promote a number of best practices in online teaching, especially within the subject areas they are responsible for. You can see a list of mentor tasks and are welcome to contact one of the mentors if you are in need of course design guidance, or even if you simply need someone to brainstorm ideas with.
